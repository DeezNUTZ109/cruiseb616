P&O Cruise B616

14-Night Iceland & British Isles Voyage | Aboard Britannia

Voyage Overview

Cruise B616 is a 14-night adventure sailing from Southampton to Iceland and the British Isles aboard the stunning Britannia.

Enjoy scenic sea days, dramatic fjords, volcanic landscapes, historic Scottish ports and vibrant Icelandic culture.

  • Departure: Southampton, UK
  • Duration: 14 Nights
  • Ship: Britannia
  • Region: Iceland & Northern Europe

Full Itinerary

Day 1 – Southampton

Boarding begins, explore the ship, enjoy sail away party, welcome dinner and evening entertainment.

Day 3 – Cobh (Cork), Ireland

Explore historic Cork, Blarney Castle, scenic countryside tours and Irish cultural experiences.

Days 6-7 – Reykjavik (Overnight Stay)

Golden Circle excursions, Blue Lagoon spa, waterfalls, geysers and vibrant Icelandic nightlife.

Day 8 – Isafjordur

Remote fjords, hiking trails, birdwatching and breathtaking natural scenery.

Day 9 – Akureyri

Visit Lake Myvatn, volcanic craters, geothermal landscapes and scenic coastal drives.

Day 12 – Kirkwall (Orkney Islands)

Discover Viking history, ancient stone circles and charming island villages.
